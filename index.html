About Me

My love to perform and entertain runs parallel with my love of technology and creative engineering. While getting my degree in digital film, I discovered my love for problem-solving and working on collective projects. Researching the most efficient way to execute a particular task, with time frame and quality in mind, brings me joy in life. After learning the value that these attributes hold in the development world along with getting some hands-on experience, I decided that computer science aligned with my ultimate goals. So, I decided to dive a little deeper into this world of web development and design.
